Three stages of a relationship ↑

  1. Curiosity: help them survive!
  2. Enlightenment: but how [you solve their problems]? (if you confuse you loose)
  3. Commitment: ask (but not too early).

Nurture the relationship:

Keep in touch (by mail for example).

Asess ↑

Are you piquing your customers’ curiosity with your website, your signature, the first pages of your proposals, and through the talking points your salespeople employ?

Are you earning your customers’ trust by enlightening them about how you can solve their problems and help them survive?

Are you inviting your customers to place an order through a complimentary and direct ask?

El problema con las computadoras

The thing about computers is that their evolution depends on the admirable, herculean efforts of a staggering number of fallible human beings, all over the world, coordinating their efforts very poorly. ^[That’s right. Somewhere under the hood of every computer game you have ever loved is a highly questionable hackjob that looks like the bits and bytes equivalent of duct tape and popsicle sticks. This is true all the way down to the internet itself, so don’t get the idea that you’re somehow safe from this hackery.]

Chelsea Troy—“Smart” is Not a Hiring Criterion
